# 01 Vision

## Project Vision
BeautyAtHome is a home beauty services marketplace focused on Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities in India, starting with Sikar as the pilot city.

## Mission
Connect verified female beauty professionals with customers who want trusted home service booking, simple discovery, and reliable service completion.

## Positioning
- Affordable
- Local trust
- Female-only professionals at launch
- Home service convenience
- India-expandable brand

## Long-Term Direction
The platform should support expansion into additional cities and nearby service categories later, without changing the core system design.

## Core Principles
- Start simple
- Build trust first
- Reduce bypass risk
- Keep operations manageable
- Preserve future scalability
