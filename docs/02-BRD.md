# 02 BRD

## Business Requirements Document

## Business Objective
Build a trusted home beauty services marketplace for Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities, starting with Sikar, to connect customers with verified female beauty professionals.

## Problem Statement
Customers in smaller cities often have limited access to reliable home beauty services, while freelancers lack a structured platform to receive bookings, build trust, and manage earnings.

## Business Goals
- Launch in Sikar as a pilot city
- Validate demand for home beauty services
- Build repeat bookings through trust and convenience
- Enable verified female professionals to earn through the platform
- Create a system that can expand across India

## Target Users
### Customers
- Women and households seeking beauty services at home
- Users who prefer convenience, safety, and verified professionals

### Professionals
- Female beauty freelancers
- Independent beauty professionals with home-service capability

### Admin
- Platform operator responsible for verification, operations, disputes, and growth

## Scope of Launch
### Included
- Beauty services only
- Female professionals only
- Customer booking via website/PWA
- OTP login
- Freelancer verification
- Booking management
- Commission tracking
- Support and dispute handling

### Excluded for Phase 1
- Mobile app
- Male professionals
- Non-beauty services
- Advanced AI automation
- Live location tracking
- Multi-city launch

## Success Metrics
- Number of registered freelancers
- Number of active customers
- Booking completion rate
- Repeat booking rate
- Cancellation rate
- Support resolution time
- Monthly commission revenue

## Business Constraints
- Initial city focus is Sikar
- Trust and safety are critical
- Operations must remain manageable in Phase 1
- Platform must avoid unnecessary complexity

## Strategic Direction
The first phase should validate unit economics, trust, and repeat usage before scaling to additional cities.
